Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to Creator Insight ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at creator-insight.vercel.app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us when you join our waitlist or use our services:

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, browser type (anonymized)
  • Social media analytics data — when you connect your accounts (read-only)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you updates about Creator Insight's launch and new features
  • Display your social media analytics within your personal dashboard
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Respond to your questions and provide support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Social Media Data

When you connect your YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook accounts to Creator Insight:

  • We access your analytics data using read-only permissions only
  • We never post, modify, or delete content on your behalf
  • We never share your analytics data with third parties
  • Only you can see your own analytics data
  • You can disconnect your accounts at any time
  • We store only aggregated analytics data — not your raw content

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database with row-level security). We implement the following security measures:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS encryption
  • Row-level security — you can only access your own data
  • API keys stored in secure server environment variables — never exposed in the browser
  • Server-side rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Input sanitization to prevent injection attacks

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for essential functionality such as keeping you logged in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — website hosting (Privacy Policy)
  • Google/YouTube API — analytics data (Privacy Policy)
  • Meta/Instagram API — analytics data (Privacy Policy)
  • TikTok API — analytics data (Privacy Policy)

7. Data Retention

We retain your email address on our waitlist until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained only while you have an active account. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from our waitlist at any time
  • Disconnect your social media accounts at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

9. Children's Privacy

Creator Insight is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
